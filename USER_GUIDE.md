## Getting Started

### First-Time Access

1. **Receive Your Credentials**
   - Your administrator will provide you with:
     - Username
     - Temporary password
     - Organization URL

2. **Initial Login**
   - Navigate to the login page
   - Enter your username and temporary password
   - Click "Login"

3. **First Login Requirements**
   - You will be prompted to change your password
   - Create a strong password (minimum requirements apply)
   - Verify your email address if required

4. **Complete Your Profile**
   - After first login, you may need to complete your employee profile
   - Fill in all required information
   - Upload any required documents

## Login & Authentication

### Logging In

1. **Access the Login Page**
   - Open your web browser
   - Navigate to your organization's EMS URL
   - You'll see the login page

2. **Enter Credentials**
   - **Username**: Enter your assigned username
   - **Password**: Enter your password
   - Click the "Login" button

3. **Forgot Password?**
   - Click "Forgot Password" link
   - Enter your username or email
   - Check your email for reset instructions
   - Follow the link to reset your password

4. **Forgot Username?**
   - Click "Forgot Username" link
   - Enter your registered email address
   - Check your email for your username

### Changing Your Password

1. **Access Password Change**
   - After logging in, go to "Change Password" from the menu
   - Or you'll be prompted on first login

2. **Update Password**
   - Enter your current password
   - Enter your new password
   - Confirm your new password
   - Click "Update Password"

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Logging Out

- Click on your profile/user menu
- Select "Logout"
- You'll be redirected to the login page

## Leave Management

### Applying for Leave

1. **Navigate to Leave Application**
   - Click "Leaves" in the navigation menu
   - Click "Apply for Leave" button

2. **Fill Leave Application Form**
   - **Leave Type**: Select from dropdown (Annual, Sick, Personal, etc.)
   - **Start Date**: Select your leave start date
   - **End Date**: Select your leave end date
   - **Number of Days**: Automatically calculated (or enter manually)
   - **Reason**: Enter reason for leave
   - **Comments**: Any additional comments (optional)

3. **Submit Application**
   - Review your information
   - Click "Submit" or "Apply for Leave"
   - You'll receive a confirmation message

4. **Track Your Application**
   - Go to "Leaves" ‚Üí "My Leaves"
   - View status: Pending, Approved, Rejected
   - See approval history

### Viewing Leave Balance

1. **Check Leave Balance**
   - Dashboard shows your current balance
   - Or go to "Leaves" ‚Üí View balance details

2. **Understanding Leave Balance**
   - **Total Allocated**: Leaves allocated for the financial year
   - **Used Leaves**: Leaves already taken
   - **Remaining Leaves**: Available leaves
   - **Financial Year**: The year period (e.g., 2024-2025)

### Viewing Leave History

1. **Access Leave List**
   - Click "Leaves" in navigation
   - View all your leave applications

2. **Filter and Search**
   - Filter by status (Pending, Approved, Rejected)
   - Filter by leave type
   - Filter by date range
   - Search by keywords

3. **Leave Details**
   - Click on any leave to view:
     - Application details
     - Approval status
     - Approver information
     - Approval date/time
     - Comments

### Editing/Canceling Leave

- **Pending Leaves**: Can be edited or canceled
- **Approved Leaves**: May require cancellation request
- **Rejected Leaves**: Cannot be edited

To edit/cancel:
1. Go to "Leaves" ‚Üí Find your leave
2. Click "Edit" (if pending)
3. Make changes or click "Cancel"
4. Confirm the action

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: After logging in, go to "Change Password" from the user menu, or you'll be prompted on first login.

**Q: Can I access EMS on my mobile device?**
A: Yes! EMS is a Progressive Web App (PWA) and can be installed on mobile devices. Look for the install prompt or add to home screen option.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For best experience, use the latest version.

**Q: Can I use EMS offline?**
A: Limited offline functionality is available through PWA features. Full functionality requires an internet connection.

### Leave Management Questions

**Q: How do I know my leave balance?**
A: Your leave balance is displayed on the dashboard and in the Leaves section.

**Q: How long does leave approval take?**
A: Approval time depends on your organization's workflow. Typically 1-3 business days.

**Q: Can I cancel an approved leave?**
A: Contact your manager or HR. Some organizations allow self-cancellation through the system.

**Q: What if I need to edit my leave application?**
A: You can edit pending leave applications. Once approved, contact your manager or HR.

**Q: How are leave balances calculated?**
A: Balances are calculated based on your allocated leaves for the financial year, minus used leaves.

### Document Questions

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: Common formats including PDF, images (JPG, PNG), Word documents, and more. Check with your administrator for specific limits.

**Q: Is there a file size limit?**
A: Yes, file size limits apply. Contact your administrator for specific limits.

**Q: Can I delete uploaded documents?**
A: Yes, you can delete your own documents if permitted by your organization's policies.

**Q: How long are documents stored?**
A: Documents are stored according to your organization's retention policies.

### Profile Questions

**Q: Can I update my profile information?**
A: Yes, you can update most of your profile information. Some fields may be restricted.

**Q: What if my information is incorrect?**
A: Update it through the profile edit feature, or contact HR if you don't have edit access.

**Q: Do I need to complete my profile?**
A: Yes, completing your profile is important for system functionality and compliance.

### Attendance Questions

**Q: How is my attendance recorded?**
A: Attendance recording depends on your organization's setup - it may be automated or manual.

**Q: Can I view my attendance history?**
A: Yes, go to the Attendance section to view your attendance records and history.

**Q: What if my attendance is incorrect?**
A: Contact your manager or HR to correct attendance records.

### Technical Questions

**Q: The page is slow or not loading**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing browser cache, or checking your internet connection.

**Q: I'm getting error messages**
A: Note the error message and contact IT support or your administrator.

**Q: Can I use multiple devices?**
A: Yes, you can access EMS from any device with internet access and a web browser.
